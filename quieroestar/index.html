× Certificate of Authenticity

A certificate of authenticity (COA) is a document from an authorized source that verifies the authenticity of the song record. While many certificates of authenticity are signed by the artist, others are signed by their managers or the record label that owns the work.

COAs usually include the artist's name, details (title, date, support, dimensions) of the work in question and, whenever possible, an image of the work.
